**Abstract**
HectX is a NAV-based tokenized product offering economic exposure to a diversified portfolio of private, pre-IPO equity stakes and reserve assets. The token represents a pro rata claim on NAV, not direct ownership of underlying companies, and does not grant voting, information, or redemption rights.

**Body**
I. Product Definition (High-Level)
I.a HectX is a NAV-based token; its reference value is tied to the NAV of a vehicle holding private equity positions plus reserve assets, net of fees and liabilities.
I.b Token holders own an indirect economic claim on the vehicle’s NAV rather than direct ownership in portfolio companies.

II. Economic Exposure And Limits
II.a NAV includes deployed private-company stakes and reserve assets; at launch it may be mostly reserves prior to deployment.
II.b Any dividends, distributions, or liquidity proceeds from portfolio companies are handled at the vehicle level and reinvested or allocated per Hecto policy.

III. Rights And Non-Rights
III.a Rights include economic exposure to NAV and permissioned transferability within compliance rules.
III.b No rights to redeem, vote, receive company information, or claim direct shareholder status in portfolio companies.

IV. Liquidity Model
IV.a No redemption rights are granted by default.
IV.b Liquidity is expected through secondary trading and discretionary buybacks; buybacks are not guaranteed.
