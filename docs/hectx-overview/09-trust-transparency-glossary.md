**Abstract**
Trust and transparency are central to HectX. The project commits to periodic disclosure of NAV, portfolio composition at an aggregated level, and governance processes. This chapter also standardizes key terms used throughout the implementation.

**Body**
I. Trust And Transparency (High-Level)
I.a Transparency is a core principle for investor confidence and accountability.
I.b Regular disclosures cover NAV, portfolio performance, reserve composition, and major portfolio events.

II. Reporting Commitments
II.a Provide regular NAV updates and periodic portfolio disclosures.
II.b Disclose valuation methodology and material changes to valuation inputs.
II.c Balance transparency with confidentiality and regulatory constraints.

III. Governance And Risk
III.a Document governance processes, risk management, and operational controls.
III.b Maintain auditability for NAV inputs, minting, and transfer restrictions.

IV. Glossary (Implementation Terms)
IV.a Accrued fees: fees earned but not yet settled, deducted from NAV.
IV.b Base reserve asset: the base asset used to hold reserves and price subscriptions.
IV.c CC: payment rail used for subscriptions; funds are converted to base reserve assets.
IV.d Circulating supply: HectX tokens issued and outstanding.
IV.e Compliance perimeter: the set of accounts and wallets approved to hold or receive HectX.
IV.f Corporate action: IPO, M&A, tender offer, or liquidity event affecting a holding.
IV.g Elastic mint fee: fee applied to subscription when market price > NAV.
IV.h Fair value: valuation used to price portfolio holdings for NAV.
IV.i GAV: gross asset value, sum of portfolio stakes plus cash and receivables.
IV.j NAV: net asset value, GAV minus fees, reserves, and liabilities.
IV.k NAV per token: NAV divided by circulating supply.
IV.l Notice: third-party data provider for private market valuation inputs.
IV.m Premium: market price above NAV; discount is market price below NAV.
IV.n Pre-mint NAV per token: NAV per token before a new subscription is added.
IV.o Prohibited person: person in prohibited jurisdictions or on sanctions lists.
IV.p Restricted person: person in restricted jurisdictions requiring additional criteria.
IV.q Reserve assets: liquid assets held pending deployment or for liquidity.
IV.r Reserve sleeve: the portion of NAV held as reserves rather than deployed in holdings.
IV.s Redemption: a right to return tokens to the issuer for NAV; not granted by HectX.
