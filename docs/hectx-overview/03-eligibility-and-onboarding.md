**Abstract**
Eligibility and onboarding are strict, jurisdiction-aware gates. HectX is generally offered to non-U.S. persons, with prohibited and restricted jurisdictions explicitly enumerated. Participation requires successful KYC/KYB, sanctions screening, source-of-funds checks, and an approved wallet.

**Body**
I. Eligibility Model (High-Level)
I.a HectX is generally intended for non-U.S. investors and is offered in compliance with international securities laws.
I.b U.S. persons are prohibited under Regulation S and cannot onboard or hold tokens.
I.c Any person in a prohibited jurisdiction, or sanctioned by U.S., U.K., E.U., or U.N. authorities, is ineligible.
I.d Entities are considered prohibited if 50% or more of ownership is held by prohibited persons.

III. Restricted Jurisdictions And Thresholds
III.a Brazil
III.a.1 Individual: financial investments > BRL 1,000,000.
III.a.2 Entity: financial assets > BRL 5,000,000.
III.b European Economic Area (EEA)
III.b.1 Individual: professional client criteria, meeting at least two of: 10 significant transactions per quarter over 4 quarters; portfolio > EUR 500,000; 1 year of financial sector experience.
III.b.2 Entity: regulated financial institutions or large undertakings meeting two of: balance sheet >= EUR 20,000,000; net turnover >= EUR 40,000,000; own funds >= EUR 2,000,000.
III.c Hong Kong
III.c.1 Individual: portfolio >= HKD 8,000,000.
III.c.2 Entity: portfolio >= HKD 8,000,000 or total assets >= HKD 40,000,000.
III.d Malaysia
III.d.1 Individual: net personal assets >= RM 3,000,000, or joint spousal assets >= RM 6,000,000, or gross annual income >= RM 300,000 (or RM 400,000 jointly) in preceding 12 months.
III.d.2 Entity: AUM >= RM 10,000,000.
III.e Singapore
III.e.1 Individual: assets >= SGD 2,000,000, or net financial assets >= SGD 1,000,000, or income >= SGD 300,000 in preceding 12 months.
III.e.2 Entity: net assets >= SGD 10,000,000.
III.f Switzerland
III.f.1 Professional clients including financial intermediaries, insurers, regulated foreign clients, central banks, public entities with professional treasury, pension schemes with professional treasury, companies with professional treasury, or large companies meeting two of: balance sheet >= CHF 20,000,000; turnover >= CHF 40,000,000; equity >= CHF 2,000,000.
III.g United Kingdom
III.g.1 Individual: certified high net worth (income >= GBP 100,000 or net assets >= GBP 250,000), certified sophisticated, or self-certified sophisticated investor.
III.g.2 Entity: investment professionals, local authorities, or large undertakings meeting two of: balance sheet >= GBP 18,000,000; turnover >= GBP 36,000,000; employees >= 250.

IV. Onboarding Workflow
IV.a Collect identity, entity, beneficial ownership, source-of-funds, and jurisdiction data.
IV.b Perform KYC/KYB, sanctions screening, and eligibility verification.
IV.c Register and approve wallet addresses; only approved wallets can receive or hold HectX.
IV.d Enforce terms acceptance and maintain auditable records of acceptance.

V. Ongoing Monitoring
V.a Periodic re-verification for sanctions, jurisdiction changes, or status changes.
V.b Freeze or block transfers for accounts that become non-compliant.
